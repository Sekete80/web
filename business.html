Business Information

How We Make Money

  • Activity fees from trail rides, pony rides, and lessons
  • Birthday party packages
  • School group trips
  • Corporate team building events
  • Merchandise sales (hats, t-shirts, souvenirs)
  • Snack and drink sales at the picnic area
  • Photography sessions

Business Expenses

  • Horse feed and veterinary care
  • Staff salaries (guides, instructors, stable hands)
  • Safety equipment (helmets, saddles, bridles)
  • Facility maintenance (fences, trails, buildings)
  • Insurance for the park and activities
  • Marketing and advertising
  • Water and electricity

Booking Process

  1. Customer browses activities on our website
  2. Customer fills out the booking form
  3. We reply within 24 hours to confirm availability and total price
  4. Customer pays upon arrival (cash or bank transfer)
  5. Customer signs a safety waiver
  6. Customer enjoys the activity with a guide

Our Customers

  • Local families with children
  • Tourists visiting Lesotho
  • School groups for educational trips
  • Corporate groups for team building
  • Birthday parties for children

Park Policies

  • Helmets are mandatory for all riders
  • All riders must sign a waiver before participating
  • Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance for a refund
  • No refunds for no-shows or same-day cancellations
  • Activities may be rescheduled due to bad weather

Future Plans

  • Expand trail network to 15 kilometers
  • Add overnight camping experiences
  • Build a small cafe at the park
  • Partner with local hotels and tour operators
  • Offer horse riding certification courses
  • Host annual horse riding competitions
